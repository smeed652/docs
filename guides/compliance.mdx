## Overview

CorrData helps operators maintain compliance with federal pipeline safety regulations:

- **49 CFR 192** - Transportation of Natural and Other Gas by Pipeline
- **49 CFR 195** - Transportation of Hazardous Liquids by Pipeline

## Compliance Dashboard

The compliance dashboard provides:

- Real-time compliance status by regulation
- Upcoming deadlines and overdue items
- Historical compliance trends
- Audit-ready reports

## Key Regulations

### 49 CFR 192.465 - External Corrosion Control

**Requirements:**
- Pipe-to-soil potential measurements at test stations
- Readings at intervals not exceeding 2 months for Class 3 & 4
- Records retained for life of pipeline

**CorrData Features:**
- Automated deadline tracking
- Mobile app for field readings
- Instant sync when connectivity available

### 49 CFR 192.463 - Interference Surveys

**Requirements:**
- Investigate stray currents
- Take remedial action where necessary
- Maintain interference bonds

**CorrData Features:**
- AC/DC interference monitoring
- Automated anomaly detection
- Integration with third-party monitoring

### 49 CFR 192.467 - Electrical Isolation

**Requirements:**
- Maintain isolation between pipeline and other structures
- Test isolation effectiveness

**CorrData Features:**
- Isolation point tracking
- Test result recording
- Trend analysis
