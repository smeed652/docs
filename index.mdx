## Welcome to CorrData

CorrData is a comprehensive pipeline integrity management platform that combines cathodic protection monitoring, risk assessment, and compliance tracking with modern AI capabilities.

## Why CorrData?

| Feature | CorrData | Traditional CIMS |
|---------|----------|------------------|
| **AI Integration** | Native MCP server | None |
| **API** | Modern GraphQL | Legacy REST/SOAP |
| **Mobile** | Offline-first PWA | Limited or none |
| **Deployment** | Cloud-native | On-premise only |
| **Risk Scoring** | ML-powered | Manual calculations |
